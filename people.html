Principal Investigator

Terry Eskenazi

Assistant Professor of Psychology · Principal Investigator

Terry Eskenazi is an Assistant Professor of Psychology at Koç University. She received her MSc in Social Psychology at the London School of Economics, and completed her PhD at the Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour in the Netherlands. She then continued research at École Normale Supérieure's LNC (Laboratoire de Neurosciences Cognitives) before joining Koç University. Her research interests lie within social cognition and neuroscience, focusing on social decision making and metacognition.

teskenazi@ku.edu.tr
Collaborators & Alumni
DÇ

Dicle Çapan

PhD · Collaborator & Alumna

Dicle received her B.A. in Psychology from Bahçeşehir University and completed her M.A. in Cognitive Science. She is mainly interested in social influences on metacognitive evaluations and memory processes, with plans to study social learning in relation to metacognition from a developmental perspective. Outside the lab, she enjoys chocolate, city escapes, concerts, movies, and books.

dcapan17@ku.edu.tr
DÇ

Dilan Çabuk-Çolak

PhD · Collaborator & Alumna · Asst. Prof. at Acıbadem University

Dr. Dilan Çabuk-Çolak is an alumna of SOMI and currently serves as an Assistant Professor at Acıbadem University. Her research focuses on social memory, and she often integrates emerging technologies such as virtual reality into her experimental work. Beyond academia, she enjoys reading and exploring new places.

dilan.colak@acibadem.edu.tr
