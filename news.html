2024

SOMI Lab at "From Self-Knowledge to Knowing Others" Workshop

Members of SOMI Lab presented their work at the international workshop "From Self-Knowledge to Knowing Others" held in Brussels, Belgium — an exciting opportunity to share our research with the European cognitive science community.

2024

Prof. Eskenazi at Koç University Brain & Evolution Symposium

Our lab coordinator, Asst. Prof. Terry Eskenazi, presented at Koç University's Brain and Evolution Symposium, bringing together researchers across disciplines to discuss the evolution of cognition.

2023

Prof. Eskenazi at COG-IST Summer School

Asst. Prof. Terry Eskenazi participated as an instructor at COG-IST's Summer School, contributing to the training of the next generation of cognitive scientists in Istanbul.
