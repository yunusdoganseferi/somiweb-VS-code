We are looking for motivated MA & PhD students to join SoMi Lab.

Who We're Looking For

We welcome applications from students with a strong interest in social cognition, metacognition, and the psychological mechanisms underlying social interaction. No specific prior research experience is required — curiosity and motivation are what matter most.

Current research areas include:

  • Illusory Truth Effect & belief updating
  • Social & collective memory
  • Gesture and metacognitive awareness
  • Advice taking and social metacognition
  • Metacognitive mechanisms in social feedback
